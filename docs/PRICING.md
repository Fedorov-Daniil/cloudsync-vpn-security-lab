# Pricing

These prices are example portfolio pricing in RUB and are not a final legal offer.

| Package | Example monthly price | Best for |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Basic | 990 RUB/month | 1 website/domain |
| Standard | 2,490 RUB/month | Up to 3 websites/domains |
| Pro | 5,990 RUB/month | Multiple services and runbooks |

## What's included

- Monitoring target definition.
- Uptime and certificate checks.
- Monthly health report.
- Incident checklist.
- Backup readiness review for Standard and Pro.
- Operational runbooks for Pro.

## What's not included

- Full SOC service.
- Endpoint antivirus or EDR.
- Full penetration testing.
- Legal compliance certification.
- Guaranteed uninterrupted availability.
- Hosting, domain registration, or paid third-party tooling fees.

## Optional setup fee

An optional one-time setup fee may apply when the client needs onboarding, baseline documentation, DNS review, or backup readiness mapping before monthly reporting starts.

## Notes and disclaimer

The service improves visibility and recovery readiness, but no monitoring or DNS security setup can guarantee full protection. Client-specific scope, access rules, and legal terms should be agreed before any paid work.
