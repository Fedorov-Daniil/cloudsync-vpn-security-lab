# Service Offer

CloudSync SafeOps is a lightweight operations service for small businesses and personal projects that need better visibility into uptime, DNS security, certificates, backups, and incident handling.

It reduces risk, improves visibility, helps detect failures faster, and supports safer recovery. It does not promise perfect availability or complete protection.

## CloudSync SafeOps Basic

For one small website or landing page.

- 1 website/domain.
- Uptime monitor.
- SSL expiry check.
- Monthly health report.
- Basic incident checklist.

## CloudSync SafeOps Standard

For small businesses with a few critical public services.

- Up to 3 websites/domains.
- DNS security recommendations.
- Uptime and certificate monitoring.
- Backup readiness review.
- Monthly report.
- Incident response checklist.

## CloudSync SafeOps Pro

For projects that need a stronger operating routine.

- Multiple services.
- Monitoring design.
- Backup/restore planning.
- Operational runbooks.
- Monthly review.
- Priority incident assistance.

## Client expectations

- The client keeps ownership of domains, hosting, and accounts.
- SafeOps uses documented, approved checks.
- Any production change requires explicit approval.
- Reports are written in practical language.
- High-risk work is scoped separately.
